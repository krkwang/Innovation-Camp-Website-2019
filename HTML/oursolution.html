Our solution

A research from 2005 shows that up to 93% of Ugandans listen to the radio at least once a week. Our solution is therefore an activity camp where children gather to share their experiences and express themselves while having fun. The children will have the opportunity to play with each other and get to know each other through games, and then during breaks they will have the opportunity to discuss environmental issues. Save the Children will provide a stand where children can interview each other and their inputs will be recorded in both audio and video.
